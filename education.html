Relevant modules

Group studies, Astrolab and Observatory Laboratory

Each of these modules involved working in groups of 4-8 to write Python programs capable of analysing astronomical datasets and thus allowing research to be conducted. It was in these modules that I discovered my passion for working with data and the satisfaction of producing results after designing analysing programs. These modules also allowed me to vastly improve my Python programming skills by allowing me to put my previous knowledge into practice, and forcing me to learn when my existing knowledge was insufficient. After completing these modules, I am now particularly confident with NumPy, MatPlotLib and Pandas.
